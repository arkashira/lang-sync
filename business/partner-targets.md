### Summary
The integration roadmap focuses on localization management and machine translation APIs that can enhance the lang-sync product's capabilities. The selected partners not only address the pain points of localization but also have strong affiliate or revenue-sharing programs, providing an additional monetization avenue for lang-sync. The integration efforts are balanced to ensure a mix of manageable and more complex integrations, allowing for a phased rollout.
