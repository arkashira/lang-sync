# Customer Journey Map: lang-sync

## Phase 1: Aware
**Trigger Event:** Developer encounters localization bottleneck in CI/CD pipeline  
**Friction Points:**  
- No awareness of automated translation solutions  
- Difficulty identifying relevant tools in crowded marketplace  
- Lack of clear value proposition from competitors  
**User Emotions:** Frustration, curiosity, uncertainty  
**Opportunities to Delight:**  
- Educational content showcasing localization pain points  
- Case studies from similar dev teams  
- Clear, jargon-free product positioning  
**Metric:** Brand awareness lift (social media mentions, website traffic from search)

## Phase 2: Consider  
**Trigger Event:** Developer researches localization tools after experiencing bottlenecks  
**Friction Points:**  
- Complex comparison matrix of competing solutions  
- Uncertainty about integration complexity  
- Concerns around translation quality and accuracy  
**User Emotions:** Skepticism, evaluation fatigue, cautious optimism  
**Opportunities to Delight:**  
- Free demo with real-world scenario simulation  
- Integration compatibility checklist  
- Quality assurance guarantees and sample outputs  
**Metric:** Time to first demo request, conversion rate from awareness to consideration

## Phase 3: Try
**Trigger Event:** Developer signs up for free trial or beta access  
**Friction Points:**  
- Onboarding complexity and learning curve  
- Initial setup configuration challenges  
- Limited documentation for edge cases  
**User Emotions:** Excitement, anxiety, experimentation  
**Opportunities to Delight:**  
- Guided onboarding flow with success indicators  
- Dedicated support during initial setup  
- Quick-start templates and best practices  
**Metric:** Trial activation rate, time to first successful translation, support ticket volume

## Phase 4: Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Team adopts lang-sync for production workflows  
**Friction Points:**  
- Scaling translations across multiple languages  
- Maintaining quality consistency  
- Integrating with existing toolchain  
**User Emotions:** Confidence, satisfaction, occasional concern about reliability  
**Opportunities to Delight:**  
- Performance optimization tips and benchmarks  
- Community forum for peer collaboration  
- Proactive feature updates based on usage patterns  
**Metric:** Adoption rate, retention rate, monthly active users

## Phase 5: Expand
**Trigger Event:** Team sees ROI and wants to scale usage  
**Friction Points:**  
- Pricing complexity for larger teams  
- Need for advanced features like glossary management  
- Coordination with other departments  
**User Emotions:** Excitement, strategic planning, potential cost concerns  
**Opportunities to Delight:**  
- Tiered pricing with clear value progression  
- Enterprise feature roadmap visibility  
- Success stories from similar organizations  
**Metric:** Upsell conversion rate, average revenue per user growth, customer lifetime value
