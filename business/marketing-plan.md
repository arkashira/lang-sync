# Marketing Plan for lang-sync

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning:** Automated translation pipeline for development teams to streamline localization services.

**Alternative Positioning:**
1. AI-powered localization solution for agile development teams.
2. Seamless multilingual development workflow for global teams.
3. Automated translation and localization for faster software releases.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profiles)
1. **Persona:** Alex, Senior Software Developer
   - **Daily Job:** Manages a team of developers working on a global product.
   - **Budget:** $5,000 - $10,000 per month for localization tools.

2. **Persona:** Jamie, Product Manager
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees product development and ensures timely releases in multiple languages.
   - **Budget:** $3,000 - $8,000 per month for localization services.

3. **Persona:** Taylor, Localization Specialist
   - **Daily Job:** Coordinates translation and localization efforts across different regions.
   - **Budget:** $2,000 - $6,000 per month for translation tools.

## Channels
1. **Channel:** LinkedIn Ads
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 - $100 per acquisition.
   - **Rationale:** Targets professionals in software development and product management.

2. **Channel:** GitHub Sponsors
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 - $70 per acquisition.
   - **Rationale:** Directly reaches developers and open-source contributors.

3. **Channel:** Tech Conferences and Webinars
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 - $200 per acquisition.
   - **Rationale:** Engages with industry leaders and decision-makers.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Introduction to lang-sync, benefits of automated translation, case studies.
- **Week 5-8:** Deep dive into features, user testimonials, comparison with competitors.
- **Week 9-12:** Success stories, advanced use cases, integration guides.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features, create marketing collateral, set up advertising campaigns.
- **D-0:** Official launch on GitHub, LinkedIn, and other channels.
- **D+30:** Gather user feedback, optimize marketing strategies, announce first updates.
- **D+90:** Expand marketing efforts, introduce new features, and scale user acquisition.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Aim for 500 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** Achieve $10,000 in Monthly Recurring Revenue by D+90.
